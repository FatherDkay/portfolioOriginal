About Me

For the last 12 years I have served the special needs community. I started as an aide in a classroom working with students with behavioral challenges. Once I completed my masters in special education I became a transition teacher, working with moderate/severe students ages 18-22. Eventually I moved from the classroom into an administrative role as the director of special education for 2 different non-public schools. Prior to my work in special education I earned a bachalors in business and worked mostly in sales. In my free time I enjoy various hobbies. I am a woodworker and a musician plying both drums and piano. I am also an avid fan of themeparks frequenting many of the parks available here in Southern California.
